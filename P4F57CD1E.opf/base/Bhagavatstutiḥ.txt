praṇidhāya manaḥ sahendriyairvidhivad vāk caśarīrameva ca |guṇabhūta mahāguṇodadhestava vakṣyāmi
guṇaikadeśatām

paramapravaro'smi vādināmanavadyaḥsusamāhitendriyaḥ |paramārthavidaprakampitaḥ prayataiḥsarvaparapravādibhiḥ

caraṇaṁ susamāptameva te susamāptavratasādhitavrataḥ |balavāṁśca samādhiravyayastava nārāyaṇaśailarājavat

puruṣaṣabha nāsti te vyathā na viṣādo na bhayaṁna ca klamaḥ |na ca te vyasanaṁ kutaḥ kalirna
ca bhūteṣukadācidakṣamā

na ca dhāvasi nātilīyase na ca santapyasi nāpihṛṣyase |satataṁ śubhameva te manaḥ satataṁmerurivācalādhipaḥ

munipuṁgava sarvadhātubhirvipulaṁ jñānamapāvṛtaṁtava |aparāhatamakṣayavyayaṁ vividheṣvāyataneṣuvartase

na ca te'sti mune kathaṁ kathā vimatirnāsti nasaṁśayaḥ kvacit |svayameva na te parāparaṁ viditaṁ
sarvamavedividyayā

priyadarśanasādhudarśanapriyasādhupriyapaṇḍitapriyaḥsamameva hi te priyāpriyaṁ satataṁprītikarastatheryase

madhurapratibhānavānasi smitavākyaḥ smṛtamānviśāradaḥ |vividānumataṁ prabhāṣase triṣu lokeṣu
ca te stṛtaṁyaśaḥ

nṛsurāsurayakṣarākṣasā bahavastvāmiha lokapaṇḍitāḥ|upagamya mune punaḥ punaḥ paripṛcchānti nacābhiṣūyase

svavacaḥ paritoṣitāstvayā naradevāḥ surayakṣarākṣasāḥ|pratiyānti vinītasaṁśayāścaraṇau vandya
ca temahāmuni

sthitamāsitamāgataṁ gataṁ śayitaṁmaunamathābhyudīritam |atha cīvarapātradhāraṇaṁ ruciraṁ gautamasarvameva
te

avilambitamadrutaṁ samaṁ svaramādhuryaguṇaiḥsamanvitam |vacanaṁ punaruktavarjitaṁ samaye vyāharasenarottama

balavānasi lokaviśrutaḥ puruṣajñaḥ puruṣarṣabhaḥprabhuḥ |na ca manyasi nāvamanyase sakalaṁ lokamimaṁsadaivatam

na ca vismayase kadācana prakṛtistheṣu calācaleṣuca |suhṛdeṣu [ca] durhṛdeṣu ca pratikūleṣvanulomavatsu
ca

paridevyamadīnanisvanaṁsrutalālārpitasannināditam |paridāhavidāhasaṁyutaṁ bhayarogajvaraśokakarṣitam

prasamīkṣya jagat samākulaṁ vividhairduḥkhaśatairupadrutam|cirarātramanāthamutsukaṁ bhavatṛṣṇāprasṛtaṁtamovṛtam

avatārya munermahākṛpāṁ vividhāṁ cātmagatāṁprabhāvatām |svayameva hi śākyapuṅgava vyathitānmocayituṁtvayocyate

ativīrya mahāvināyakapravarāṇāmanuvādināṁ vara|na ca te sadṛśaḥ kuto'dhikastriṣu lokeṣvapinaiva
vidyate

pratighānunayā na santi te na ca te santi muniparisravāḥ |anurodhavirodhavigrahāḥ satataṁ suvrata
naivanaiva te

apahāya mune priyāpriye sukhaduḥkhe vi[ṣamaṁ samaṁ tathā]aratiṁ ca ratiṁ viparyayannupaśāntaścarasīha
saṁyatah

vyasane na ca nāma nirmanā na ca nāmonnamasepraśaṁsayā |ayaśaśca yaśaśca te samaṁ samamākruṣṭamathāpivanditam

ativākyamatho titikṣase paruṣaṁpāpajanairudīritam |samarāgragato viṣāṇavān bhṛśamuktāniva kuñjaraḥśarān

suvacastvamṛṣi vacaḥ kṣamaḥ sudurukteṣvapinābhiṣūyase |samameva ca varttase mune paribhāṣāsu
śubhāsuvākṣu ca

satataṁ ca varārha pūjyase naradevāsurayakṣarākṣasaiḥ|ṛṣibhiśca sadā mahātmabhirna ca te vikriyatesthiraṁ
manaḥ

pravaro'hamito na manyase'tyavaro'hamito namanyase |sadṛśo'hamito na manyase trividhā mānavidhā
nasanti te

dhṛtimān samaloṣṭakāñcanaḥ samavaiḍuryakaṭhallaśarkaraḥ|tṛṇakāṣthasamaṁ mahāmune carasīdaṁ hi
sadevakaṁjagat

himabhāskararaśmisaṁgamācchiśiroṣṇaṁ pavanaṁsamudvahan |adhivāsayase nagendravanna ca te pravyathatesthiraṁ
manaḥ

śayanāsanapānabhojanaṁ vividhaṁcīvaramuttarādharam |adhivāsayase nagendravanna ca te pravyathatemune
nirāsravam

na ha śocāsi nātha nāsti me na ca te santi muneparigrahāḥ |asito'si suvākyanirmamḥ parimukto
vividhairupadravaiḥ

na ca lābhamavāpya hṛṣyase tadalābhācca layaṁna gacchasi |avamānamatho titikṣase na casammānamihabhinandasi

kṣatajopamamagracandanaṁsurabhūmandarasānusambhavam |asayo niśitāḥ śarāśca te na vikurvanti
manaḥkadācana

na kathāṁ kathayasyanarthikāṁ na ca yādurjanasevitā kathā |na [ca lābhakathā na] sāmiṣā na ca
yā nānumatā mahātmabhiḥ

pravivekakathāḥ sukhāvahāḥ praśamaṁ pravadantikevalam |kathayasyatidevatāḥ kathāḥ kathitā yā
vinayantikilviṣam

madhurāṇi ca saṅgatāni ca svabhinītāni casāravanti ca |vacanāni mune prabhāṣase jagadarthāya
viniṣcitānica

abudhā viparītadarśanāścapalāḥ sāhasikāḥpriyanvitāḥ |piśunāḥ paruṣā śaṭhāśca ye bhagavaṁstaiḥ
sahasaṅgataṁ na te

aśaṭhā ṛjavaśca ye narāḥ śucayaḥ satyaratājitendriyāḥ |satataṁ ca samīkṣyakāriṇo bhagavaṁstaiḥ
saha saṅgataṁtava

dharmajña nayajña pudralajña tvāṁ vande ṣaḍabhijñasarvadaiva |kṣetrajña mune parāparajña tvāṁ
vande śirasānayānayajña
